System Overview

Understanding Addiction

Smartphone addiction is a growing concern affecting mental health. This core system actively monitors and flags high-risk behavioral patterns in registered subjects.

We collect comprehensive metrics like screen time and app frequency, utilizing feature engineering to train predictive engines for precise intervention.

Model Development
